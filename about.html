Definition

Familial Chylomicronemia Syndrome (FCS) is an ultrarare inherited disorder of lipid metabolism. In FCS, triglyceride‑rich particles (chylomicrons) accumulate due to impaired clearance, leading to very high triglyceride levels. (Replace with your preferred wording/references).

Why an international project?

Because FCS is extremely rare, international collaboration is essential to pool cases, harmonise methods, and generate robust natural‑history and clinical insights.
